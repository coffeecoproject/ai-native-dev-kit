# Industrial Pack Candidates

Use this directory for legacy industrial baselines before they are promoted into `industrial-packs/`.

Candidate material may be incomplete, duplicated, or still coupled to older naming. Promotion requires:

- inventory of source files
- split into `primary-platform`, `capability`, and `risk-overlay` packs
- normalized names
- `pack.md`
- `pack.json`
- `scripts/check-industrial-pack.mjs` passing
- purity review for project-specific facts
- at least one dogfood pass with a real or representative project

Do not treat candidate packs as official AI Native Dev Kit workflow assets.
