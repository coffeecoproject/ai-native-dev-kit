# Auth And Permission Checklist

- [ ] Identity and session lifecycle are documented.
- [ ] Role, scope, tenant, or ownership model is documented.
- [ ] Allowed and forbidden behavior is verified.
- [ ] Protected resources are enforced server-side where applicable.
- [ ] Credential and secret boundaries are reviewed.
- [ ] Privileged access has audit evidence.
- [ ] Exceptions and residual risks are accepted by a human.
