# Auth And Permission Boundary Baseline

## Required Coverage

- identity, role, scope, tenant, or ownership model is documented
- allowed and forbidden behavior is verified
- protected resources are enforced server-side where applicable
- credential, session, or token lifecycle boundaries are documented
- privileged access has audit trail expectations
- exceptions and residual risks are recorded in project evidence

## AI Boundary

AI may prepare evidence and checks, but must not approve permission expansion, credential handling, secrets, privileged access, or residual risk.
