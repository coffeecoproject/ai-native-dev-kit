# Value Transfer Safety Baseline

## Required Coverage

- value movement state model and invariants are documented
- duplicate-submit and idempotency behavior are verified
- permission and approval boundaries are documented
- reconciliation and audit trail evidence exists
- rollback, compensation, or mitigation strategy is defined
- exceptions and residual risks are accepted by a human

## AI Boundary

AI may prepare evidence and checks, but must not approve production value movement, financial risk, privileged adjustments, or residual risk.
