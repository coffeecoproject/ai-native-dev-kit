# Mini Program Auth And Permission Checklist

- [ ] WeChat login state and session binding are covered when login is touched.
- [ ] openid, unionid, tenant, role, or resource scope boundaries are documented when used.
- [ ] Platform permission prompts are covered when camera, location, phone number, album, clipboard, subscription message, or similar capabilities are touched.
- [ ] Denied, revoked, expired, and forbidden states are covered where applicable.
- [ ] Personal data collection is minimized and has a privacy impact note when touched.
- [ ] Human approval exists for changed permission boundaries or privacy-impacting exceptions.
