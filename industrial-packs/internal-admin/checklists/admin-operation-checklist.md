# Admin Operation Checklist

- [ ] Role and permission matrix is documented.
- [ ] Forbidden and scoped-resource behavior is verified.
- [ ] Privileged operations produce audit evidence.
- [ ] Production data boundaries are reviewed.
- [ ] Destructive or irreversible operations have confirmation and mitigation.
- [ ] Operator impact and rollback evidence exists.
- [ ] Exceptions and residual risks are accepted by a human.
