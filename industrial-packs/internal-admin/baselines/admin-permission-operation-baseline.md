# Admin Permission And Operation Baseline

## Required Coverage

- role and permission model is documented
- forbidden states and scoped-resource boundaries are verified
- privileged operations have audit trail requirements
- destructive or irreversible operations require confirmation and mitigation
- production data access boundaries are documented
- operator impact and rollback paths are reviewed

## AI Boundary

AI may prepare evidence and checks, but must not approve privileged operations, permission expansion, production data access, or residual risk.
