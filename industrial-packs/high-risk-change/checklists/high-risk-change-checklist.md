# High-risk Change Checklist

- [ ] Risk classification is explicit.
- [ ] Approval scope is documented.
- [ ] Blast radius and affected surfaces are documented.
- [ ] Verification matrix covers affected surfaces.
- [ ] Rollback, mitigation, or recovery plan exists.
- [ ] Production config, secrets, and regulated-data impacts are reviewed where relevant.
- [ ] Monitoring and incident response evidence exists.
- [ ] Exceptions and residual risks are accepted by a human.
