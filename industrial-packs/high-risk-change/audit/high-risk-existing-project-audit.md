# High-risk Existing Project Audit

## Audit Areas

- production config and secret boundaries
- destructive or irreversible operations
- regulated or sensitive data paths
- migrations and compatibility-breaking changes
- security-sensitive areas
- rollback, recovery, monitoring, and incident response readiness

## Output

Record:

- high-risk areas requiring human approval
- missing evidence for risk classification or rollback
- stop conditions
- recommended first controlled slice
