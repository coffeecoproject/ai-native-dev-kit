# High-risk Change Baseline

## Required Coverage

- risk classification and approval scope are explicit
- blast radius and affected surfaces are documented
- rollback, mitigation, or recovery path is defined
- verification matrix covers all affected surfaces
- production config, secret, and regulated-data impacts are reviewed where relevant
- monitoring and incident response evidence exists

## AI Boundary

AI may prepare evidence and checks, but must not approve high-risk execution, production release, destructive behavior, secret changes, or residual risk.
