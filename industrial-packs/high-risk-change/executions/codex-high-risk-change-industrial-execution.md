# Codex High-risk Change Industrial Execution

## Before Implementation

- read `AGENTS.md`
- resolve selected profiles and industrial packs
- classify risk, affected surfaces, blast radius, rollback, monitoring, and approval scope
- stop for human approval before implementing L3 risk or irreversible behavior

## During Implementation

- keep changes scoped to the approved task card
- preserve rollback and recovery options where possible
- update verification matrix, monitoring, incident, and recovery evidence
- record exceptions rather than silently weakening the baseline

## Before Final Report

- run available verification
- include risk classification, blast radius, rollback, monitoring, and approval evidence
- list residual risks and stop conditions
