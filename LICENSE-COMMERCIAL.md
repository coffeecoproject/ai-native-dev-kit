# Commercial License Boundary

This file explains the commercial-use boundary for this repository. It is not legal advice and does
not replace or override [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md).

## Default Rule

The public license allows personal, educational, and non-commercial use with attribution.

Commercial use is not permitted without prior written permission.

## Uses That Require Prior Written Permission

- Commercial product delivery.
- Paid customer project delivery.
- Commercial consulting or service delivery.
- Resale or paid redistribution.
- Bundling this material into paid templates, courses, toolkits, services, or implementation
  packages.
- Copying generated assets, templates, checkers, or workflow materials into a customer project as
  part of paid delivery.

## Internal Evaluation

A company may review the repository internally as a non-commercial evaluation. That evaluation does
not grant the right to use the material in paid delivery, customer work, commercial operations, or
resold packages.

## Requesting Permission

Commercial licensing is available on request. Get prior written permission before using the
material commercially.

## No Legal Advice

This document is a project policy explanation. For legal interpretation, consult qualified legal
counsel.
