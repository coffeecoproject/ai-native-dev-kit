## Signing Boundary

Signing configuration, keystore integration or bounded local preparation,
manifest settings, and local bundle/APK verification are technical preparation
owned by Codex. They require project evidence, secret-safe handling, and
internal review, not user technical approval. Codex must not invent a secret,
expose keystore material, or claim that a local package was uploaded.

Ask for an unavailable external account fact when it cannot be inspected. Ask
for exact consent only before accessing or changing a real Play account,
uploading signing material or a package, distributing to a testing track, or
releasing to production.
