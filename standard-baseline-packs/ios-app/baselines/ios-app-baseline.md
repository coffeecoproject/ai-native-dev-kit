# iOS App Baseline

## Scope

Use this baseline when iOS app structure, navigation, state ownership, build settings, simulator checks, or device behavior are in scope.

## Expectations

- Keep feature, view, state, and service boundaries visible.
- Record minimum platform, runtime, signing, and configuration assumptions as confirmed or pending.
- Verify changed flows in simulator, preview, device, or a documented substitute.
- Keep App Store, privacy, capability, and release decisions separate from ordinary app baseline work.

## Boundary

This baseline does not approve App Store submission, production readiness, privacy decisions, release decisions, or target-project writes.
