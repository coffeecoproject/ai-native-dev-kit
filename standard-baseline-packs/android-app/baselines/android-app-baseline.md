# Android App Baseline

## Scope

Use this baseline when Android app module structure, navigation, state ownership, Gradle build, emulator checks, or device behavior are in scope.

## Expectations

- Keep app module, feature, state, and service boundaries visible.
- Record SDK, Gradle, emulator, and configuration assumptions as confirmed or pending.
- Verify changed flows in emulator, device, or a documented substitute.
- Keep Play Store, privacy, permission, and release decisions separate from ordinary app baseline work.

## Boundary

This baseline does not approve Play Store submission, production readiness, privacy decisions, release decisions, or target-project writes.
