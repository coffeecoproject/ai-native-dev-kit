# Request 130: Guided Delivery Baseline

## Human Summary

Add a guided delivery baseline so IntentOS upgrades stay aligned with human judgment, bounded AI authority, honest claims, and visible assumptions.

## Request

Create a complete 1.3.0 phase that formalizes:

- outcome baseline
- product baseline
- claim control
- assumption register
- deterministic checks
- examples and bad fixtures
- release evidence

## Non-goals

- Do not build Safe Launch / Delivery Readiness in this phase.
- Do not add external reviewer automation.
- Do not make claim reports mandatory for every task.
- Do not change target-project write behavior outside existing manifest-driven asset installation.

## Human Decision

Status: Approved for implementation.

Scope: 1.3.0 guided delivery baseline only.
