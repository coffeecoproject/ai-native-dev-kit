# 1.7.0 Known Limitations

- First Delivery Walkthrough is a simulated or recorded process path, not production validation.
- Adoption Trial Reports only prove what their evidence supports.
- Empty target projects are allowed; reports are created only when a walkthrough or real trial is recorded.
- The checker cannot inspect private conversations that were not recorded as artifacts.
- The checker cannot prove that implementation code is correct, secure, compliant, or production-ready.
- The example under `examples/1.7-first-delivery-walkthrough/` is not real project adoption evidence.
- Subagents remain advisory. They do not approve release, risk, payment, privacy, security, legal, compliance, migration, or production use.
- BL2 and industrial packs remain selected-only and human-confirmed.
