# IntentOS 1.111.1 Known Limitations

- Semantic rules cover registered and discovered effective guidance plus known
  generated paths; they cannot prove the meaning of every future natural-
  language variation.
- Compatibility schemas and historical records retain legacy field names.
  Current consumers must continue interpreting them through the current
  responsibility contract.
- Technical evidence can still reveal a genuine missing business or external
  fact. IntentOS cannot infer facts that the project and authoritative sources
  do not contain.
- This patch does not replace or collapse the existing task, review,
  verification, apply, release, and completion source systems.
