# IntentOS 1.78.0 Known Limitations

- Completion Evidence Gate checks recorded artifacts; it does not execute tests.
- It can confirm source-chain readiness, but it does not prove product correctness.
- It does not prove production or real-environment behavior.
- It depends on upstream artifacts being generated honestly and checked by their own validators.
- It is not a release, deployment, commit, push, or production approval system.
