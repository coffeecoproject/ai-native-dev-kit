# 1.60.0 Known Limitations

- Release Handoff Packs are read-only planning and evidence packages.
- Provider-specific execution remains out of scope.
- Production, store, mini-program, migration, DNS, payment, permission, and production config actions remain human-owned or external-system-owned.
- Handoff packs do not prove a release is correct, safe, or accepted by a platform.
- Project-specific SOPs can be referenced, but this version does not validate live provider state.
