# IntentOS 1.108.0 Release Record

## Theme

Business Universe Coverage.

## Delivered

- conditional Task Governance routing for category-omission risk;
- one project- and task-bound Business Universe Coverage artifact covering
  categories, participants, origins, processing paths, lifecycle branches,
  selection points, consistency relationships, stable verification scenarios,
  path provenance, and required proof strength;
- evidence-backed LOW-to-MEDIUM monotonic reclassification while preserving the
  targeted MEDIUM review policy;
- deterministic deep-project discovery with trusted partial-scan cursor,
  inventory identity, resume-state digest, and stale/tampered resume rejection;
- exact evidence binding through Business Rule Closure, Change Impact
  Coverage, Plan Review, Verification Plan, Test Evidence, Completion Evidence,
  and public finish routing;
- Challenger review for high-risk or broad completeness claims;
- platform-neutral impact and verification mapping that keeps Web, worker,
  Mini Program, backend, internal-admin, iOS, Android, storage, and generic
  project-native paths distinct;
- generated-project distribution for generic, Web, iOS, and Android starters,
  with executable Mini Program, backend API, and internal-admin profile smokes;
- zero-experience behavior: Codex performs technical scanning and asks only for
  unavailable business or external facts.

## Allowed Claims

- IntentOS can detect supported category-omission risk signals and require an
  evidence-bound business universe before affected tasks can close.
- A required scenario, lifecycle branch, project path, or proof-strength mapping
  cannot be silently dropped from strict downstream consumers.
- Tasks without omission risk remain on the existing lightweight path.

## Forbidden Claims

- Coverage proves every possible business category exists.
- A successful coverage report proves implementation or product correctness.
- Business Universe Coverage replaces Unified Closure or authorizes release.
- Codex may invent unavailable legal, tax, policy, or external authority facts.

## Boundaries

This release adds an internal evidence source, not a public workflow. It does
not write implementation files, execute tests, approve completion, or authorize
release or production.

## Evidence Status

This release counts as complete only after the strict scenario-preservation,
resume, platform/profile, evidence-authority, generated-project, Manifest,
Review Context, IntentOS self-check, full repository verify, and diff checks all
pass for the same candidate worktree.
