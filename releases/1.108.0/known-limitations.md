# IntentOS 1.108.0 Known Limitations

- Routing and candidate discovery are deterministic and project-evidence based;
  they cannot prove that an undocumented business category does not exist.
- Missing technical scan evidence remains Codex-owned work. Missing business or
  external authority facts can still block readiness until supplied.
- Challenger evidence reduces omission risk but is not independent proof when
  it uses the same model and project context.
- Historical Business Rule, Impact, Verification, and Completion artifacts stay
  readable under their historical contracts. Only an exact current
  `NOT_REQUIRED_WITH_REASON` projection may remain on the compatibility path;
  historical artifacts cannot satisfy a required strict 1.108 Universe binding.
- Platform mapping uses project-owned paths and active profile evidence. Unknown
  placement remains technical inspection work and cannot be guessed from an
  industry term.
- This release does not execute tests or production behavior and cannot claim
  absolute business correctness.
