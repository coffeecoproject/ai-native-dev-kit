# 1.3.0 Known Limitations

## Human Summary

1.3.0 improves product and claim governance, but it does not prove production adoption or launch readiness.

## Limitations

- No Safe Launch / Delivery Readiness implementation.
- No production evidence.
- No external GPT/API reviewer automation.
- No hook automation.
- No industrial pack promotion.
- No default BL2.
- Claim control is deterministic and conservative.
- Assumption Register is not mandatory unless uncertainty affects decisions or reports.

## Required Human Judgment

Humans still decide:

- launch readiness
- production risk
- customer delivery
- release approval
- legal or commercial claims
- next phase scope
