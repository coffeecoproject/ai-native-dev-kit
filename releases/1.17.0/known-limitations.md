# 1.17.0 Known Limitations

- Baseline Decision Cards are recommendations only.
- The resolver reads local project signals; it does not replace human confirmation.
- BL2 remains candidate-only unless evidence and human decisions are recorded.
- Industrial packs remain draft and selected-only.
- The checker validates recorded cards; it does not prove a real project is ready.
- Production-sensitive and governed projects still require adapter or controlled-apply review before writes.
- The guided entry does not approve security, privacy, compliance, payment, finance, tax, HR, migration, release, production, or irreversible data decisions.
