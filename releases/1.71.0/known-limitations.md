# Known Limitations - 1.71.0

- Adoption assurance is read-only and does not apply migrations.
- It does not replace project-owned release, CI, hook, business, data, security, compliance, legal, tax, finance, HR, secrets, payment, or production authority.
- Simulation proves workflow routing, not product correctness.
- Local real-project calibration is optional and must not become public hard-coded evidence.
- `VERIFIED_ACTIVE` is an adoption assurance state, not a production launch approval.
