# IntentOS 1.52.0 Known Limitations

- Guided Closure does not prove product correctness.
- Guided Closure does not create missing reports automatically.
- Guided Closure does not approve implementation, commit, push, release, production, CI, hooks, migration, payment, permission, legal, tax, security, privacy, or compliance decisions.
- The `finish` command summarizes selected read-only signals; maintainers can still run low-level strict checks for CI and audit evidence.
- Existing historical reports remain compatible and are not migrated automatically.
