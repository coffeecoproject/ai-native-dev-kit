# Release 1.16.0 Known Limitations

## Human Summary

1.16.0 makes BL2 industrial packs deeper, but it does not make them stable or production-proven.

## Limits

- All industrial packs remain `draft`.
- BL2 remains selected-only and requires explicit human confirmation.
- Pack files are not project evidence.
- Simulated examples are not real production validation.
- Risk overlay checks are keyword and evidence-shape checks; they are not financial, legal, compliance, security, privacy, tax, HR, or production approval.
- Mobile examples do not prove App Store, TestFlight, Play, signing, provisioning, or device-matrix readiness.
- Existing governed projects still require read-only mapping and controlled apply planning.

## No Authorization

1.16.0 does not authorize:

- target-project writes
- implementation
- release or production
- security, privacy, legal, or compliance approval
- payment, finance, HR, tax, or migration approval
- residual risk acceptance
