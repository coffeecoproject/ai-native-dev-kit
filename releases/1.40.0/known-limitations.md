# Known Limitations: 1.40.0

- Approval records are evidence, not execution.
- The checker validates Markdown records. It does not authenticate a real human identity outside the repository.
- The checker requires a plan hash but does not recompute it yet.
- Approval records do not replace Controlled Apply Readiness.
- Approval records do not approve release, production, hooks, CI, migrations, secrets, payments, security, privacy, compliance, legal, or industrial packs.
- A future controlled apply runner is still only a candidate roadmap item.
