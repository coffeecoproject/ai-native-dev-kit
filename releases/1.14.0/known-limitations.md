# Release 1.14.0 Known Limitations

- Standard packs are draft.
- Only three standard packs are included in 1.14.0.
- Auth, data, observability, supply chain, accessibility, mobile, and Mini Program standard packs are intentionally deferred.
- `baseline-packs` is an umbrella recommendation, not activation.
- Standard selection does not approve implementation.
- Industrial overlays remain selected-only and human-approved.
- Real production validation is not claimed.
