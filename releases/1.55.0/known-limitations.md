# Release 1.55.0 Known Limitations

- Launch Review View is a read-only view. It does not execute deployment or release.
- It can identify missing launch review surfaces, but it cannot prove production correctness.
- It depends on Unified Closure and Safe Launch evidence. Weak upstream evidence still requires human review.
- It does not replace project-owned release, rollback, incident, app-store, compliance, security, privacy, finance, tax, or legal processes.
