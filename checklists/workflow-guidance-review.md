# Workflow Guidance Review Checklist

- The card starts with a Human Decision Summary.
- The card has exactly one Delivery Path State.
- Plain mode does not expose internal workflow jargon.
- The recommendation is one next step, not a task bundle.
- The card asks no more than 3 questions by default.
- High-risk cards ask no more than 5 questions.
- Unknown usage defaults to conservative handling.
- The card does not approve target-project writes.
- The card does not modify CI or install hooks.
- The card does not delete, move, or archive documents.
- The card does not change task state.
- The card does not approve implementation, release, production, or high-risk domain decisions.
- Distance To Useful Use says what is still missing before other people can use the project.
