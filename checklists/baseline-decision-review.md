# Baseline Decision Review

- [ ] Human Summary is short and plain-language.
- [ ] Project State explains new, existing, governed, production-sensitive, dirty, or unknown status.
- [ ] Platform and scope are separated from assumptions.
- [ ] Platform states distinguish selected, present-needs-confirmation, present-inactive-or-deferred, and not-detected profiles.
- [ ] BL0, BL1, or BL2 recommendation is clear.
- [ ] Current safe action is separated from target candidate level.
- [ ] Standard packs are recommended by platform and scope, not selected all at once.
- [ ] Industrial packs are candidate-only unless BL2 and evidence are confirmed.
- [ ] Backend is not forced for frontend-only or Mini Program projects.
- [ ] Human Decisions Needed has no more than 5 questions unless explicitly justified.
- [ ] Safe Next Actions are concrete and bounded.
- [ ] Boundary states that the card does not approve target-project writes.
- [ ] Boundary states that the card does not approve implementation.
- [ ] Boundary states that the card does not approve release or production.
- [ ] Boundary states that the card does not approve security, privacy, compliance, payment, migration, or other high-risk decisions.
- [ ] Existing governed projects do not recommend overwrite.
- [ ] Production-sensitive projects do not recommend direct init/update.
- [ ] Dirty worktrees stop before write actions.
