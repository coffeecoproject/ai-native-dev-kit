# Real Adoption Trial Review Checklist

- Is the trial read-only?
- Are target writes explicitly recorded as `No`?
- Was target git status checked before and after when a real target was inspected?
- Is the primary adoption mode valid?
- Are secondary risk tags recorded?
- Are existing governance sources mapped before any bridge proposal?
- Does the bridge layer mode use `NO_WRITE_MAP`, `DOCS_ONLY_BRIDGE`, `THIN_OPERATIONAL_BRIDGE`, or `NOT_PROPOSED`?
- If `AGENTS.md` is proposed, does the report mention existing `agent.md` or explain its absence?
- Does the report keep real project evidence local or sanitized unless approved?
- Are production, release, security, privacy, compliance, and legal claims bounded?
- Are subagents closed or skipped?
- Does the next step require human approval before target writes?
