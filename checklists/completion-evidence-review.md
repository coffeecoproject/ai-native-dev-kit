# Completion Evidence Gate Review

- Confirm the report is read-only.
- Confirm Business Rule Closure is recorded and ready.
- Confirm Verification Plan is recorded and ready.
- Confirm Test Evidence is complete and task-bound.
- Confirm Execution Assurance is `VERIFIED_DONE`.
- Confirm all source artifacts use the same task ref.
- Confirm `can_claim_complete` is `Yes` only when all gate checks pass.
- Confirm the report does not approve commit, push, release, production, or product correctness.
