# Controlled Native Adoption Review Checklist

- Human Summary is plain language.
- Source chain includes source evidence, not only the 1.81 adoption card.
- Governance maturity is classified.
- Recommendation is bounded and review-only.
- Weak governance is not left partial without a repair path.
- Strong governance is not pushed toward native assets without value.
- User decisions avoid internal subsystem names.
- Boundaries say target writes, native apply, CI/release changes, production,
  and full adoption are all not approved.
- Risk, verification, and rollback implications are recorded.
