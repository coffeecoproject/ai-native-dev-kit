# Beginner Entry Review Checklist

- The card can be understood without knowing internal workflow command names.
- The card states what Codex understood.
- The card recommends one safe path.
- The card asks at most 3 human questions by default.
- The card separates what Codex can do next from what it must not do yet.
- Technical routing is evidence, not user burden.
- The card does not authorize writes, apply, implementation, release, production, CI, hooks, document changes, task-state changes, baseline packs, industrial packs, or high-risk decisions.
- If writes may be needed, the next step is plan-first and approval-gated.
