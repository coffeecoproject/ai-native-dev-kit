# Product Baseline Review Checklist

Use this checklist when changing Dev Kit workflow, docs, release wording, automation, generated-project behavior, or industrial pack maturity.

## Outcome

- Does the change help users move from an idea to risk-matched delivery?
- Does it reduce user burden instead of adding ceremony?
- Does it keep the first response understandable for non-expert users?

## Authority

- Does a human keep judgment, approval, and risk acceptance?
- Are reports, review packets, goal cards, and subagent outputs kept as evidence rather than approvals?
- Are governed, dirty, unknown, and production-sensitive projects still read-only or plan-first?

## Evidence

- Are allowed claims and forbidden claims stated?
- Is evidence status explicit?
- Are known limitations preserved?
- Is simulated evidence clearly separated from real-project and production evidence?

## Automation

- Does the change avoid default external API or hook automation?
- Does it avoid multiple writers?
- Does it avoid default BL2 or default all-pack installation?

## Assumptions

- Are important assumptions listed with evidence and confidence?
- Are low-confidence or high-risk assumptions routed to humans?
- Are inferred facts kept out of formal baselines?
