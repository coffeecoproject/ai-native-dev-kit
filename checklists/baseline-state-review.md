# Baseline State Review

- [ ] Baseline states use only allowed values.
- [ ] No-code baselines are `PROPOSED`, `PENDING_CONFIRMATION`, or `EVIDENCE_REQUIRED`.
- [ ] `CONFIRMED` items cite evidence or human-confirmed source of truth.
- [ ] Draft industrial packs are not claimed stable or production-ready.
- [ ] `BASELINE_READY` is not claimed while key items are proposed or evidence-required.
- [ ] Human decisions are listed for pending or high-impact items.
