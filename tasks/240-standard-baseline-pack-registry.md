## Goal

Implement 1.14.0 as an additive standard baseline pack registry release.

## Allowed Scope

- Standard baseline pack registry and draft pack assets
- Standard baseline resolver and checkers
- CLI, manifest, generated-project assets, CI, docs, release evidence

## Forbidden Scope

- No production readiness claim
- No license change
- No stable pack promotion
- No broad platform content expansion beyond the first three standard packs
- No target-project write approval through recommendations
